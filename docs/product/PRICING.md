# Pricing — NJZ RAT-OS

**Status:** Active (Phase 0 placeholder; Phase 2 finalization)
**Owner:** Product

## Pricing Tiers

### Free

All seven modules' core functionality. Default PolyCo.World decorations. Basic analytics. Ad-free.

Rationale: the integration thesis requires real free utility. A crippled free tier sabotages the cross-module loop we depend on for retention.

### Premium

| Period | Price | Discount vs MSRP |
|--------|-------|-------------------|
| Launch promo (Months 2–6) | **$39.99/year** | 33% |
| Standard | **$59.99/year** | — |
| Monthly (if offered) | $7.99/month | — |
| Lifetime (limited Phase 2 offer) | $199 one-time | — |

**Premium unlocks:**

- Detailed cognitive analytics + 30/60/90-day trends + brain age + CSV/JSON export.
- Premium soundscapes (Deep Canvas advanced themes, 8-hour Sleep Painting, custom frequency builder).
- Distraction blocker advanced enforcement (uninstall protection, settings lock).
- Writing Space premium exports (EPUB, DOCX, custom cover images).
- PolyCo.World cosmetic catalog access + seasonal exclusives.
- Cross-device sync (5+ devices).
- Priority support.

### Cosmetic Packs (Phase 3+)

One-time purchases for PolyCo.World items not tied to module progression:

| Pack | Price |
|------|-------|
| Seasonal theme bundle | $4.99 |
| Premium decoration set (~30 items) | $9.99 |
| Hero outfit + aura | $2.99 |

### Team / Enterprise (Phase 4)

- $7/seat/month, minimum 10 seats.
- Includes everything in Premium plus admin dashboard, anonymized team analytics, SSO.
- Pilot pricing negotiated case-by-case.

## Why $39.99 Launch Promo

Anchors against the documented $245–305/year savings vs separate-app substitution while remaining accessible to the Primary tier ($30–50/yr tolerance). The promo period (Months 2–6) compounds the early-adopter narrative that drives Reddit + Product Hunt traction.

## Why $59.99 Standard

Sits between Lumosity ($59.99), Endel low end ($59.99), and Calm ($69.99). At parity with single-function competitors despite delivering 7+ modules. Positions RAT-OS as *the* category-redefining option.

## What We Won't Do

- Energy systems, hearts, or "wait to play" mechanics in the free tier.
- Loot boxes or gacha.
- Real-money trading of PolyCo.World items between users.
- Dark-pattern subscription cancellation flows.
- Hiding the free tier behind premium messaging.

## Refunds

- Annual: prorated refund within 14 days of charge, no questions asked.
- Lifetime: 30-day refund window.
- Cosmetic packs: non-refundable but transferable to another account (Phase 4+).
